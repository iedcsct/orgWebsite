The Innovators Premier League is a Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) programme that aims to raise awareness of IEDC and its activities among students, develop a competitive and entrepreneurial culture among innovators, and generate a startup pipeline.
IPL is a series of events held at various levels where you can participate to get points and reach your ultimate destination, IEDC SUMMIT.ğŸ…ğŸ†
